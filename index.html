about

Hey! I'm Mayu. I am 18 years old originally from the Sacred Valley of the Incas in the Peruvian Andes. Technically speaking, I don't live anywhere at this moment, not at least a place I can call home. I'm travelling across the US and I might settle in Europe in the Fall.

Until recently, I was a professional downhill mountain bike racer. My athletic career came to an abrupt halt during the 2022 political protests in Peru, when a severe training injury left me unable to walk for ten months. Laying in bed during that recovery, I realized I needed to build a future beyond chasing seconds on a clock. That pivotal moment forced me to let go of my athletic past and sparked a deep dive into technology.

Today, my focus is on the intersection of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and defense/national security technology. I am fascinated by how breakthrough systems can be deployed to mitigate the effects of conflict or prevent it entirely.

Currently, I’m working on a project, an intelligence layer platform to map supply chain networks and vulnerabilities for the US semiconductor industry. The project is currently undergoing a pre-seed investment round. Later this summer, I’ll be joining a Y-Combinator Fintech startup as a strategy and AI intern.

When I’m not coding , skiing, biking, or "lost" [as in travelling] somewhere around the globe, you can reach me at mayu.unda.valencia@icloud.com.

Always happy to connect with new people.
