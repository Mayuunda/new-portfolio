What I believe and why I build

Since I was a little kid I've been curious about warfare and technology. Yes, I know how that sounds but bear with me.

When I was first given access to the internet at age 5, apart from the natural interest toward Michael Jackson and Plants vs Zombies, there was something that caught my utter attention---WWII and the Manhattan Project. I was amazed by the insane scale of the conflict, the decimation of Europe, evil rising almost to total control over Europe and the incredible efforts by the Allies to terminate the conflict. We hoped for no bloody Conflict to ever spark out again, but just a couple decades later we saw Vietnam and Korea. Even more now, Eastern Europe faces the largest and bloodiest conflict since WWII in the frontlines against the Russian Federation.

But "why" is the question. Haven't we learned from history? Why agression is still persistent from certain bad actors in our world? Unfortunately answering those questions with accuracy is no easy task. If something we must take upon a challenge is the fact that as unfortunate as it is, we will never be 100% able to avoid conflict, but what we can do is to be best prepared for when it happens so we cannot only prevent the aftermath of it, but rather end it quickly.

That begs the question, if we are able to develop such technologies, could we prevent conflict altogether? Well, that is the goal and the future I envision. We don't avoid wars by dropping our weopons and stopping all operations of military technologies improvements, but rather by incrementing the edge over our adversaries to the point that combating us would be to the eyes of anyone "irrational" due to the unprecendented power we have to counterattack any offense.

Yes, I know reader, it doesn't sound pretty. But what assures us that the next country in Putin's agenda is not going to be Poland? That next time it will not only be Hamas, but the Islamic Replubic of Iran and Hezbollah attacking Israel? Or even worse, China anexing Taiwan where all our technological infrastructure depends on? How can we ensure the safety of our way of life if bad actors continue to find ways to harm us? Let's take action to put ourselves first and at the edge of technology.
